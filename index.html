    • About:

    Ballspielverein Borussia 09 e.V. Dortmund, commonly known as Borussia Dortmund, BVB, or simply Dortmund, is a soccer club based in Dortmund, Germany. They currently play in the top tier of the German football league system, the Bundesliga. Their stadium is called the Signal Iduna Park, but most fans call it the Westfalenstadion. It can contain 81,365 people inside the stadium, making it Germany's largest. Their fanbase is widely regarded as some of the most enthusiastic in the world. They have won 9 league titles, the latest one being in 2013. They are currently being managed by the Swiss Lucien Favre, who has coached them since the 22nd of May 2018. They are also widely known for being the only association football club in the world to have stock shares. The CEO of this prestigious football club is Hans-Joachim Watzke. Their captain is the 30 year old Marco Reus, in his 7th consecutive season for the club.
